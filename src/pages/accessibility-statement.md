This statement applies to this portfolio website.

I am committed to making this website accessible to as many people as possible, including people with disabilities.

## Conformance status

This website aims to conform to WCAG 2.2 level AA. It has been self-tested using manual review and screen readers (NVDA with Firefox and Chrome, VoiceOver with Safari), but has not been through a formal third-party audit, so I cannot claim full conformance.

## Known limitations

I am not aware of any current accessibility issues. If you encounter a barrier, please let me know using the contact details below.

## Feedback and contact

If you have trouble accessing any part of this site, please email [patricija.dev@gmail.com](mailto:patricija.dev@gmail.com). I'll do my best to respond promptly and fix any issues I can.
